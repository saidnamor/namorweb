About Us

About NAMOR T&M Co.

Founded in 2025, NAMOR T&M Co. is a team of specialized engineers providing metrology, calibration, testing, and electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) services. We combine expertise in instrumentation, RF systems, and international standards to deliver reliable, traceable, and practical engineering solutions tailored to each project.

Our services support industries requiring accurate measurements, regulatory compliance, and high technical confidence, with capabilities ranging from calibration and test support to EMC engineering, documentation, and consulting.

Why Choose Us

  • Experience with ISO 17025, IEC 61000 and MIL-STD-461 standards

  • Strong background in EMC and RF engineering

  • Professional instrumentation and traceable measurements

  • Personalized technical support

  • Flexible solutions for standard and custom test applications
