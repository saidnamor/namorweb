Test & Measurements

Calibration Services Performed Through Accredited Third-Party Laboratories

We provide calibration services through qualified third-party laboratories for the following measurement quantities:

  • Pressure – gauges, transmitters, manometers, and pressure calibrators.

  • Temperature – thermometers, RTDs, thermocouples, and temperature indicators.

  • Dimensional – calipers, micrometers, dial indicators, and precision measuring instruments.

  • Force – force gauges, load cells, and testing machines.

  • Mass – weights and balances.

  • Torque – torque wrenches and torque transducers.

  • Electrical – voltage, current, resistance, capacitance, and related electrical measurements.

  • Time & Frequency – timers, frequency counters, oscillators, and reference standards.
