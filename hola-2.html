MIL-STD-461 Engineering Support

NAMOR T&M Co. provides engineering support for projects requiring electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) evaluation in accordance with MIL-STD-461 requirements. Our services are focused on helping manufacturers and integrators prepare equipment (systems and subsystems) for military and aerospace EMC testing and improve test readiness.

Scope of Support

  • Test plan review and compliance guidance

  • Pre-compliance EMC measurements

  • Emissions troubleshooting (CE / RE)

  • Susceptibility assessment (CS / RS)

  • RF power and field measurement support

  • Instrumentation setup and calibration assistance

  • Technical documentation and reporting

Experience

Our engineering team has practical experience with EMC and RF systems, including work related to MIL-STD-461, with particular focus on radiated immunity, conducted susceptibility, and RF measurement techniques.

 

Important note

NAMOR T&M Co. provides engineering support and pre-compliance evaluation. Formal MIL-STD-461 qualification or certification must be performed by an accredited laboratory and accepted by the customer or contracting authority.
